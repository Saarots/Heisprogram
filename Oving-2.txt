An atomic operation?

A semaphore?
-A semaphore is a variable or abstract data type that is used for controlling access, by multiple processes, to a common resource in a parallel programming or a multi user environment.

A mutex?
-Mutual exclusion refers to the requirement of ensuring that no two concurrent processes are in their critical section at the same time

A critical section?
- A critical section is a piece of code that accesses a shared resource (data structure or device) that must not be concurrently accessed by more than one thread of execution
